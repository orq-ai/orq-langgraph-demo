# Menu Book

## About this document

This menu book lists every dish currently offered across the delivery service's partner restaurants, along with its cuisine, category, base price, calorie count, and allergen list. Support agents and operations analysts should treat this document as the canonical source for dish-level facts — if a customer or colleague asks what is in a dish, which allergens it contains, or how many calories it has, the answer lives here.

Dishes are grouped by cuisine. Allergen labels follow the 14 regulated allergens defined in the Allergen Labeling Policy. "None" means the dish contains no regulated allergens.

## Notes for customer support

- Allergen information is the customer's responsibility to check. Support agents must **never** advise a customer that a dish is "safe" — direct them to the allergen list above and to the Allergen Labeling Policy for cross-contamination guidance.
- Prices shown are the base price at our partner restaurants. Per-restaurant premiums may apply (up to +25% for premium locations).
- Dishes marked as vegetarian or vegan reflect the ingredients only — cross-contamination in shared kitchens is possible. See the Food Safety and Hygiene Policy §3 for details.
